About

Achievement (formerly Unscripted) has been around since WotLK. We are first and foremost a semi-hardcore Mythic raiding guild. However, we have a great guild community of raiders as well as friends and family members, and it is not uncommon for there to be multiple scheduled group events in any one week. If you wait around long enough, achievement runs can be expected as the guild is home to many dedicated achievement hunters.
